WORDPILOT v8.2.2 — 2000-WORD LEXICON BETA

1. English bank
- Replaced the former 1000 synthetic context cards with 1000 additional,
  distinct vocabulary records.
- Total bank: 2000 unique records (1997 single words + 3 essential phrases).
- Preserved the reviewed IDs 1–1000 and replaced IDs 1001–2000.

2. Learning metadata
- Every record has a Turkish-readable pronunciation field.
- Every record has an A1–C2 field.
- Every record has synonym, antonym and word-family status.
- High-confidence semantic links use Princeton WordNet 3.0 and point to WordPilot IDs.
- 161 records have direct synonym links, 171 have direct antonym links and 127 have direct family links.
- Direct antonyms do not exist for every natural-language word; such records
  explicitly say that no direct antonym is available.

3. Interface
- Library groups are now “1–1000 Temel Çekirdek” and
  “1001–2000 Genişletilmiş Sözlük”.
- Added a visible lexical-bank summary.
- Custom ranges and 50-record pagination remain active.

4. Progress migration
- IDs 1001–2000 previously represented context cards. Their old status,
  history and flags are cleared once so they are not attached to unrelated
  new words. Progress for IDs 1–1000 is preserved.

5. Editorial status
- The first 1000 records remain the reviewed core.
- Records 1001–2000 are marked commercial_safe=false and still require human
  editorial review of Turkish gloss, CEFR estimate and pronunciation before
  a final commercial release.
