WORDPILOT v8.2.2 TESTER GUIDE

Check these areas after deployment:
1. Open Kelimeler and confirm that 2000 unique records are shown.
2. Select 1–1000 Temel Çekirdek, then 1001–2000 Genişletilmiş Sözlük.
3. Test a custom interval such as 50–60 and 1951–2000.
4. Open Eş Anlamlı Quiz and Zıt Anlamlı Quiz.
5. Open a word information panel and inspect pronunciation, CEFR,
   synonym, antonym and family fields.

7. Confirm that old progress for IDs 1–1000 remains present.

- Incorrect Turkish gloss
- Incorrect Turkish-readable pronunciation
