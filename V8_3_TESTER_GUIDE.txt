WORDPILOT v8.3.0 TESTER GUIDE

CONVERSATION COACH
1. Open Konuş > Conversation Coach.
2. Confirm that ten scenarios are visible.
3. Open Airport, Restaurant and Job Interview.
4. Confirm that the role, goal, mission list and useful phrases change.
5. Send at least three typed answers and confirm that one mission is
   completed after each meaningful answer.
6. Finish the session and inspect the report.
7. Restart the scenario and confirm that the scenario history and mission
   progress are reset.
8. Test microphone input in Chrome or Edge on desktop and mobile.
9. Confirm that scores change gradually rather than making extreme jumps.
10. Confirm that Mira replies are spoken when automatic speech is enabled.

PRONUNCIATION LAB
1. Test Kelime, Cümle and Senaryo target modes.
2. Record one correct reading and one deliberately incomplete reading.
3. Confirm that Accuracy, Completion, Fluency and Recognition values appear.
4. Confirm that missing target words are highlighted.
5. Confirm that the extended metrics appear in pronunciation history.

REGRESSION
1. Open Academy and start a lesson.
2. Open Reader and select a story.
3. Open the 2000-word library and test a custom interval.
4. Confirm Google and e-mail authentication screens still open.
5. Confirm Firestore/App Check requests still work after deployment.

CLOUD AI NOTE
The default GitHub/Hosting package uses the structured local scenario coach.
The generative cloud coach requires a separate Firebase Functions deployment,
Secret Manager configuration and an explicit aiEnabled=true change.
